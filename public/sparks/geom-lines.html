Line Angles — direction ratios in 3D!

Watch the angle between two 3D lines update instantly!

A 3D line's direction is described by three numbers (a, b, c) called Direction Ratios. To find the angle between two lines, we use the dot product formula. Parallel lines have angle 0°, perpendicular lines have angle 90°!
